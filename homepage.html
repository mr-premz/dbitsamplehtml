Welcome to Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions (DBGI) Dehradun

15+ Years of Scaling The Zenith Of Excellence

Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions (DBGI), established in 2005, aims to provide value-based, high-quality professional education for the benefit of society and the nation. Driven by its commitment to provide the box full of opportunities for professional and personal development, DBGI has become the Best Engineering College in Dehradun Uttarakhand.

Awarded for Best Placement Engineering College in Dehradun, Uttarakhand By MHRD

The vibrant array of cultures, voices, and unique perspectives together makes DBGI a dynamic place to learn, teach, and thrive. Being a quality-driven educational institute, it provides more than 50 Under Graduate and Post Graduate programs that give students the edge in their careers. The courses cover a broad spectrum: Engineering, Management, Computer Applications, Commerce, Hotel Management, Ayurveda, Architecture, Design (Graphic, Interior, Product), Mass Communication, Fashion Designing, Polytechnic, Agriculture, Forestry, Horticulture, Microbiology, Tourism, Hospital administration, Nursing, and Pharmacy.


Spread across eight different schools under its umbrella, namely Dev Bhoomi Institute of Technology (DBIT), Dev Bhoomi Institute of Pharmacy & Research (DBIPR), Dev Bhoomi Medical College of Ayurveda And Hospital (DBMCAH), Dev Bhoomi School of Architecture & Design (DBSAD), Dev Bhoomi Institute of Management Studies (DBIMS), NCHMCT Approved â€“ Dev Bhoomi Institute of Hotel Management (DBIHM), Dev Bhoomi School of Business Management (DBSBM), Dev Bhoomi Institute of Polytechnic (DBIP), the institute has always been devoted towards excellence in teaching, research, and innovation, making it the training ground for leaders and future professionals from different streams. Because of its approach to assist and equip the students for their holistic development, giving them a strong foundation for a successful future, DBGI has been maintaining its position amongst the top engineering colleges in Uttarakhand.
